ABOUT US

Saugat Cleaning Services is a janitorial service-based company, family-owned and operated since 2016. The owner, Purna Baniya is involved in janitorial service business since he immigrated to Vancouver, BC in 2010 from the Bhutanese Refugee Camp in Nepal. After spending two decades in the refugee camp, Baniya family is grateful to be welcomed by Canada and proud to be serving lower mainland to deliver janitorial needs.

Saugat Cleaning Services is fully insured and bonded. Our employees are Building Services Worker certified and possess work experience in janitorial services over 8 years. We provide service 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and stresses on our goal to deliver quality service that fits customers schedule and is within their budget.
