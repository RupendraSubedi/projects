ABOUT US

Saugat Cleaning Services is a janitorial service-based company, family-owned and operated since 2016. The owner, Purna Baniya is involved in janitorial service business since he came to Vancouver, BC in 2010 from the beautiful country Nepal. After spending more than two decades of his lifetime in Nepal, Baniya and his family is grateful to be welcomed by Canada and proud to be serving lower mainland to deliver janitorial needs

Saugat Cleaning Services is fully insured and bonded. Our employees are Building Services Worker certified and possess work experience in janitorial services over 8 years. We provide services 7 days a week all year around and stresses on our goal to deliver quality service that fits customers schedule and is within their budget.
